Distillation Benefits

Model distillation is a technique that creates smaller, faster models while maintaining performance by:

  • Transferring knowledge from a larger "teacher" model to a smaller "student" model
  • Reducing parameters and computational requirements
  • Enabling deployment on resource-constrained devices
  • Faster inference time for real-time applications
